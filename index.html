Who Are We?

Sky's The Limit assists the planet by mitigating the conditions of climate change, granting individuals the ability to better their community.

What we do?

Sky's The Limit offers customers supermarkets and restaurants to order vegetarian cuisine from. We also reveal eco-friendly activities that pique users' interests.

Join Us!

Join us in order to unlock exclusive discounts through our unique star point system. This ensures your contributions towards sustaining earth are met with the necessary ingredients to spice up your life!

About Carbon Footprint

Did you know approximately two and a half million pounds of carbon dioxide is emitted into the air every second. Together we can minimize our carbon footprint by taking part in consuming plant-based produce. We can also be a part of initiatives that bolster eco-friendly events. Remember together a safer future is plausible.
